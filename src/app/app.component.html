What is PAM?

required
Success is required; on failure all remaining modules are run, but the request will be denied.
requisite
Success is required, and on failure no remaining modules are run.
sufficient
Success is sufficient, and if no previous required module failed, no remaining modules are run.
binding
Success is sufficient; on failure all remaining modules are run, but the request will be denied.
